My Journey

My passion for cancer biology began eight years ago as a patient. This experience sparked my commitment to perform translational research with direct clinical impact.

As a member of the Franco Lab at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia, I focus on molecular drivers of pediatric cancer, with a recent emphasis on genomics and transcriptomics. Our work combines clinical molecular profiling with in vitro experimentation to identify therapeutic targets and dissect tumor biology.

Every experiment is driven by one goal: advancing our understanding of cancer biology to develop more effective, personalized treatments for patients and families who need them most.
